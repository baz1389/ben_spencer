Ben Spencer

Web Developer | Geologist | Music Enthusiast

I’m a Boston-based web developer with a strong geology background. I have received a B.Sc in Geoscience and a M.Sc in Hydrogeology while working in both the mineral exploration and environmental remediation industries. After dabbling in coding in my free time, I decided to make a career change into web development and enrolled in General Assembly’s immersive web development course. As a full-stack web developer, I am excited to be in an industry that requires me to constantly be learning and evolving. I look forward to utilizing my strong problem solving capabilities, love for learning, and collaborating with a team with similar goals and values.
